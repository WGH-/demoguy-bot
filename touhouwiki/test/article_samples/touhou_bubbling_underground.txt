== Notes ==
* Track 8 is a parody of [[Wikipedia:Michael Jackson|Michael Jackson]]'s song "[[Wikipedia:Thriller (song)|Thriller]]". Considering the [[Wikipedia:Thriller (music video)|music video for that song]], it fits Orin very well.
== Overview ==
Concept album, containing drama tracks and an overarching plot featuring Marisa and Nitori staying out late in Gensoukyou, read as a type of fanfic in the style of a musical play by inhabitants of Gensoukyou.
The "Touhou Bubble" mentioned throughout the album, is a parody of the [[Wikipedia:Japanese asset price bubble|Japanese Bubble]].
According to [http://www.iosysos.com/20090123-toho10pv/ an entry of the IOSYS blog], this arranger has placed importance on song, performance and voice in order to express a narrative.
